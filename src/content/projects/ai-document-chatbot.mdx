# AI-Enhanced Document Chatbot

Most "chat with your PDF" tools are toys. This one was built for people who actually work with contracts, grants, and research — where being wrong has consequences.

GPT-4 handles the reasoning. A vector database handles the retrieval. Citations make the answers auditable. It is the kind of tool you can point a lawyer or grant officer at without holding your breath.
