# AI Receptionist

A voice agent that answers the phone, understands what the caller actually wants, books an appointment, and only escalates when it should.

Used in healthcare practices and small businesses to absorb the high-volume, low-judgment work that humans burn out on, so the human team can focus on actual conversations.
