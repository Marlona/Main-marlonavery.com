# AImpact

I started AImpact because most people meet Generative AI as either a press release or a tweet — neither of which prepares them to actually use it.

## Microsoft Azure OpenAI Bootcamps

AImpact was contracted by Microsoft to architect and lead Azure OpenAI bootcamps, focused on OCR + Azure AI Document Intelligence and the surrounding tooling (Jupyter, Azure AI Studio). The bootcamps lifted engineer certification pass-rates by 30% and shepherded teams from ideation to production deployment.

## Google + Dream Machine Innovation Labs — AI Library Project

Commissioned by Dream Machine Innovation Labs, in partnership with Google, to author the curriculum and headline workshops for the AI Library Project — a five-city tour delivering Generative AI training to underserved communities.

## The aggregate

Across 80+ workshops and 10,000+ professionals trained, AImpact has been the bridge: live, hands-on, plain-English training that turns AI anxiety into AI capability. Average productivity lift across attendees: **40%**.
